Human-Computer Interactions

CPSC 581: Human-Computer Interaction II is the advanced HCI course at the University of Calgary. I took it in winter 2020.

In it, I practiced techniques for designing interactive human-computer interfaces. The course consisted of four projects, created respectively using Microsoft Visual Studio 2017, Android Studio, Arduino, and HTML/CSS/JavaScript.

This class emphasized creative thinking while pushing for more practical outcomes. It stressed the design process: sketching, critiquing and refining, and then implementing.
