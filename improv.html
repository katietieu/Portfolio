Improvising the Future

Exploring the use of improvised theatre for HCI design


Improvising the Future is a CHI-style research project done at the University of Calgary under the supervision of Dr. Lora Oehlberg.

In it, I explore various improv game categories, identify those with potential compatibility with hot topics in HCI, and handpick four games out of these categories to adapt for a particular HCI topic. The full details of my work can be found below.
